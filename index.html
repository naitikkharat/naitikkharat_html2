In the rapidly advancing field of autonomous vehicles, my project stands out as a testament to innovation and practical application. Leveraging Arduino microcontrollers and ultrasonic sensors, I've engineered a self-driving car that epitomizes the future of transportation. By integrating sophisticated algorithms and real-time data processing, the car navigates its environment with precision, autonomously avoiding obstacles and adapting to changing road conditions.

This project not only showcases my technical prowess but also demonstrates the immense potential of open-source hardware platforms like Arduino in revolutionizing transportation systems. With a commitment to pushing the boundaries of technology, my self-driving car project represents a significant step towards safer, more efficient, and sustainable mobility solutions for the future.

ChainVote: Blockchain based Voting System

My project focuses on revolutionizing the democratic process through the implementation of a blockchain-based voting system. By harnessing the power of distributed ledger technology, we aim to address longstanding issues of trust, transparency, and security in traditional voting systems. Through our innovative solution, each vote cast is cryptographically recorded on a tamper-proof blockchain, ensuring the integrity and immutability of the electoral process.

With features such as decentralized consensus mechanisms and end-to-end encryption, voters can participate in elections from anywhere in the world with confidence, knowing that their votes are securely and accurately counted. Our blockchain-based voting system not only enhances the integrity of elections but also promotes inclusivity, accessibility, and civic engagement, ultimately strengthening democratic principles and fostering trust in the electoral process.
